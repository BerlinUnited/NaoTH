Field Viewer

This dialog shows the soccer field with the perceived location of the robots.

  • Receive: Receives field drawings.
  • Clean: Cleans all drawings on the field
  • Select Field: Select different field sizes for the view.
  • Rotate: Rotate the field
  • Collect: Dont clear old drawings
  • ExportOnDrawing: Saves the field as png image after something is drawn. The png images are saved in the RobotControlGUI folder.
  • Trace: TODO
  • Slider: Creates contrast to make some drawings more visible.
  • Fit field to view: double click on the field resizes and centers the view so that everything is visible. Activating the fit to view button (crossed double arrow) enables the auto-zoom of the field canvas. With that, on resizing and rotation of the view the field canvas gets resized/rotatet accordingly.
