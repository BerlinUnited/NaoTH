Image Viewer

This dialog shows images received from roboter.

  • Receive Top: get top camera image
  • Receive Bottom: get bottom camera image
  • Receive Drawings: enables drawings on the images
  • stretch image: stretches image to maximum size while respecting the aspect ratio setting
  • keep aspect ratio: defines whether the aspect ratio of the image should be kept or not
  • Fade out: fades out the image when drawings are activated
  • Right Click - Save Image: Save current image from one camera
